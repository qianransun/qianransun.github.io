Rules of Go

  • Go is played on an square board by two players who use black and white stones. Black moves first.
  • A move consists of either a pass, or placing a stone of your color on an empty intersection, except as stated in the suicide or ko rules.
  • (Capture rule) If, after you have played your stone, any of your opponent's groups have zero liberties then you remove the stones of these groups from the board.
  • (Suicide rule) If, after you have played your stone and captured any enemy pieces, any of your groups have zero liberties then your move was illegal.
  • (Ko rule) Full board repetition with same player to move is forbidden.
  • (The end of the game) When both players pass successively the game ends.
